Design a mobile-first UI for an AI language learning app called “AudioFlash.”

The goal of the app is to help users learn real conversational phrases using AI-generated audio flashcards grouped by topic.

The design should feel modern, minimal, and calming, similar to Duolingo, Headway, or modern language learning apps.

Focus on clean typography, large touch targets, and strong audio interaction.

Use a light theme with soft colors, rounded cards, and subtle shadows.

Primary accent color: warm orange or coral (for energy and speaking practice).

Font style: modern sans-serif (similar to Inter or SF Pro).
